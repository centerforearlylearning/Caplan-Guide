Advanced Counters

Children at this point in development have a solid grasp on the basics of counting and are developing a more advanced understanding that allows them to see the patterns involved in the counting process. Children can consistently count up to 20 and may be discovering the repeating pattern withing each decade (e.g., 20, 30, 40), allowing them quickly learn how to count up to 100.
