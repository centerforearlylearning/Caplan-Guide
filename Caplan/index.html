Developmental Guide for Early Mathematics Activities

The Center for Early Learning at Purdue University aims to bridge research and practice by providing caregivers with research-based activities that match children's developmental level

How to identify individualized math activities for your child.

The goal of this guide is to help match activities to your child's current developmental level. First, ask your child how high they can count and then select the number below!

Once you have selected the number, you will see research-based activities that are most appropriate for children at the same point in development!

How high can your child count?
  • 0 to 4
  • 5 to 9
  • 10 to 14
  • 15 or higher
