Beginner Counters

Children at this point in development are beginning to understand how the numbers in the count follow a structured order, much like they learn the ABCs. The goal at this stage is to help children gain familiarity with important math concepts using different number games and songs.

Click on the domain below to learn more about where your child is in development and what activites will be most helpful!

Target skills

  • Verbal Counting
  • Cardinality
  • Story Problems
  • Number Naming
